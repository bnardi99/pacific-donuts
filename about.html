About Us

Pacific Donuts was founded in 1999. Ever since we've strived to bring you the most delicious donuts! We aren't only known for our famous donuts, but also our locally-roasted coffee. Come visit us anytime at your nearest location!

CEO

Lidia Strauss, Pacific Donuts CEO, oversees all operations, marketing and development for the brand. Lidia earned a B.S. in accounting and marketing from the University of Stanford and an MBA from the University of Southern California. Having worked in over 40 different markets around the world, Lidia has always been passionate about giving back to the local communities.

Staff

At Pacific Donuts we have one of the most experienced and prepared staff memebers you will ever find. They are dedidacted and passionate about what they do. Our priority remains customer satisfaction, supplying you with our delicious donuts and ensuring you have access to our services on-the-go!
